About Me

"I need a team of Evans following me around and helping me do tasks."
-Tucker Koepp, coworker

I'm Evan Evers, a software developer with a passion for game programming. I love helping people bring their ideas to life. I'm deeply committed to lifelong learning, and have supplemented my Bachelor's in Computer Science with a wide variety of projects.

I recently finished an internship with IndieCade, an organization which supports independent game development. Over 5 weeks, me and a team of interns put together a game for IndieCade's Climate Jam 2024. As the team's only C++ programmer, I developed all core functionality for the game, as well as assisting with UI and frontend. Our project was ranked "Best Overall" out of 48 entries.

Last year, I completed a Bachelor's in Computer Science at Biola University. I graduated Cum Laude as a First Gen student, and was privileged to serve as the founding President of Biola's Computer Science Club.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually practicing piano, guitar, and bass guitar. I'm a huge nerd about video games and music, and I love to hike.
