About Me

"I need a team of Evans following me around and helping me do tasks."
-Tucker Koepp, coworker

I'm Evan Evers, a software developer with a focus in backend. I love helping people bring their ideas to life. I'm deeply committed to lifelong learning, and have supplimented my Bachelor's in Computer Science with a wide variety of side projects.

I recently finished an intership with IndieCade, an organization which supports independent game development. Over 5 weeks, me and a team of interns put together a game for IndieCade's Climate Jam 2024. As the team's only C++ programmer, I developed all core functionality for the game, as well as assisting with UI and frontend. Our game ranked Xth overall out of 48 entries.

Earlier this year, I completed a Bachelor's in Computer Science at Biola University. I graduated Cum Laude as a First Gen student, and was priviledged to serve as the founding President of Biola's Computer Science Club.

When I'm not coding, I enjoy playing piano, guitar, and bass guitar. I'm a huge nerd about video games and music. I'm big on hiking, and I love to discover unusual pieces of art.
